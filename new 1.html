ABOUT SRI UNIVERSITY

The institute was founded in 1985 by Prof. D.Sridhar. It was initially known as the Institute of Computer Science and Technology. Sridhar founded the institute to introduce computer education to the Thanjavur District. In 1988, the institute received affiliation from Anna University to offer postgraduate diploma programmes in computer applications.Thereafter it expanded to include an Arts and Science College, College of Engineering and Technology, College of Education, Research Institution, ands Polytechnic College.
